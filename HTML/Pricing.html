Frequently Asked Questions

How does free minting work?

Simply like a cast on Farcaster that contains the MuseFrame. Your mood will be analyzed and you'll receive a unique 512px NFT minted directly to your wallet on Base - completely free!

What's the difference between SD and HD?

SD (512px) is perfect for social media and casual collecting. HD (2048px) offers museum-quality resolution, optional animation, and commercial usage rights - ideal for serious collectors and creators.

Can I upgrade from SD to HD later?

Yes! You can mint an HD version of any mood you've already created for 0.001 ETH. Your original SD NFT will remain in your wallet.

What can I do with commercial rights?

HD NFT holders have full commercial rights to their artwork. Use it for merchandise, profile pictures, marketing materials, or any other commercial purpose without restrictions.

How is my mood calculated?

Our algorithm analyzes your recent Farcaster activity including posting times, engagement patterns, and interaction types to generate a unique visual representation of your current vibe.
